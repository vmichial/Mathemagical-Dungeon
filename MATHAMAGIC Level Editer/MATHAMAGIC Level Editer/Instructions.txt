Scroll with the arrow keys.

Press pgup and pgdn to shift up and down the z-axis.

Use the number keys to set the type of tile or object based on the edit mode.

Press ~ to switch between editing tile types and objects.

Press b to activate brush mode (edit tiles by scrolling).

Press e to export the text version of the map.

Press i to import the text of another map and edit that one.

Press j to export the map as javascript code.

Enjoy life and have fun!
